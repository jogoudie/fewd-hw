Eats & Drinks

Wet Dog Tavern

Wet Dog has a beautiful patio and outdoor area. In the winter, it’s tented so it’s cozy on a cold day. We visited recently with friends who were visiting from New York with their mini australian shepherd. I spotted about a dozen other dogs who were coming and going throughout the day. They also have a giant connect four board in the back.
